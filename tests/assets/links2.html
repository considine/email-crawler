Healthypoints

Healthypoints is a platform K-op developed for the folks at enFocus in South Bend, Indiana. It's a healthcare application that strives to reduce emergency room costs by encouraging high-risk patients to interact & share their healthy habits. Some examples of the capabilities we included in this website:

  • Social media functions (creating profiles, posting, commenting, messaging, sharing images etc)
  • Integration with android and iOS applications (the former was created by our team)
  • Data analysis (with HIPAA compliancy)

RoseHarrington

Rose Harrington is an eCommerce website that sells thousands of pieces of artwork. Our client originally ran a successful Etsy shop before K-op helped her create her own platform to expand. She is able to:

  • List art with custom uploaded pictures, prices and descriptions
  • Modify collections (groupings of listings), site photographs, and offer discounts
  • Track sales and collect payments

Aquatech World

Aquatech World is an online resource database written both in English and Mandarin. The database allows scientists around the world to access aquaponics and aquaculture datasets. The site's features include:

  • Splashpage for marketing purposes where users can sign up and pay our client directly
  • Custom content management interface for creating data graphics
  • An optimized database indexed by 'tags' that is searchable by clients
  • The Aquatech-World team is happy to field questions about their site development: admin@aquatech-world.com

PaTaKa

PaTaKa is a concussion diagnosis Android application that the K-op team developed for the Notre Dame Computer Science Department. Some functionalities include

  • Voice recognition software that converts speech to text and analyzes speed and correctness
  • A data visualization panel that scores the user relative to other users of the same age group
  • Several types of tests that each contribute to an understanding of the user's mental state against their baseline

Boxing TL;DR

Boxing TL;DR Garrity McOsker is a professional boxer and aspiring boxing journalist. He hoped for a simple blogging platform, but K-op helped him envision and implement several components that have been instrumental to his growing user base

  • A rich text editor where he can create personalized articles (with 'save draft' and 'publish' options) and mail newsletters to his subscribers
  • An admin panel where he can manage users, track article views, and add podcasts that appear on his frontpage
  • A custom image interface that allows him to position and crop featured images, adapting to different browser widths
